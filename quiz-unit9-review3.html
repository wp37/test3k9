"La Tomatina is an unusual festival in Buñol, a small town in Spain. It takes place on the last Wednesday of August every year. Thousands of people come to take part in a fun fight with tomatoes. Before the fight begins, people try to climb a greasy pole to get a ham at the top. Then, water cannons signal the start of the tomato fight. People throw bags of tomatoes at each other for about one hour. Although everyone gets very messy, they all have a great time. After the fight, the streets are cleaned with water, and people enjoy eating paella – a traditional Spanish rice dish. However, you should be careful because the tomatoes can sting your eyes!"
